Mumma,

There are things I've never quite found the words for — but I hope this little journey told you some of them.

Every memory on this map has your warmth woven into it. You are the reason I know what home feels like. The reason I know how to love, how to laugh, how to keep going when things get hard.

You never asked for credit. You just showed up — every single day — quietly holding everything together, including me.

I am so endlessly grateful that of all the mothers in the world, I got you.

Happy Mother's Day, Mumma.
With all my love, always.
